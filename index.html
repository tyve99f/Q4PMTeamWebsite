Welcome to Awful Authors

Here you will need to play with friends in order to come up with strange stories!

Since it is collaborative, every player will write a sentence for a story.

The only issue: You can only read the previous person’s sentence!

Game Info

Awful Authors is played by 3-8 people.

On the first round, everyone will write a title with a sentence after.

On the second round, a new person will write a sentence for the story.

They will only be able to see the first sentence.

This will continue to happen until every player has written a sentence in every story.

After the last round of writing, they will vote on stories until the very best one is selected.

Let Your Inner Author Out!

Awful Authors is a fun and bizarre game

This means that the stories you and your friends could end in very confusing and amusing ways.

Sit back, relax, and enjoy the goofy sentences you and your friends will make!

*Please keep in mind that there are banned words to keep this game family friendly.
